Prepping is easy

All we need is a flat surface with an outlet nearby to set up our products and tools.

Hair

For blowouts, make sure you have damp hair (we recommend washing about 10-15 min beforehand). For dry styling and updos, second-day hair is best.

Makeup

For best results, we suggest cleansing and moisturizing your skin before we arrive.

Nails

Please be sure your nails are free of any hard gels or acrylics (we are unable to remove them at this time). We can remove shellac or soakable gels for a $10 fee.
