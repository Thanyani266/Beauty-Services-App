Prepping is easy

All we need is a flat surface with an outlet nearby to set up our products and tools.

Hair

For blowouts, make sure you have damp hair (we recommend washing about 10-15 min beforehand). For dry styling and updos, second-day hair is best.

Makeup

For best results, we suggest cleansing and moisturizing your skin before we arrive.

Nails

Please be sure your nails are free of any hard gels or acrylics (we are unable to remove them at this time). We can remove shellac or soakable gels for a $10 fee.

FAQs

Question: Do all Vhutudzi memberships automatically renew?

Answer: Yes, all Glamsquad memberships automatically renew on a monthly basis after any prepaid time has ended. Prepaid time may include 1 month, 3 months, or yearly.

Question: What is the Glamsquad membership?

Answer: The Glamsquad membership is the perfect way for you to get your favorite blowouts for less on a monthly basis. Plus, you can enjoy exclusive members-only perks such as a 10% discount on additional hair, nail and makeup services.

Question: How do I purchase the Glamsquad membership?

Answer: Under the Services menu on Glamsquad.com or in the Glamsquad app, click Membership. Add the Membership option to your cart and check out to purchase.
